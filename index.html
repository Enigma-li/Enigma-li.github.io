Short Bio

I am now a Postdoc Research Associate of Smart Geometry Processing Group at University College London (UCL) under the supervision of Prof. Niloy Mitra. Before that I got my Ph.D. degree from the University of Hong Kong and my supervisor is Prof. Wenping Wang.

Currently I am interested in Sketch-based Modeling, Deep Learning, 3D Vision, and Medical Image Analysis.
